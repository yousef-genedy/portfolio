I build backend systems that stay reliable under growth.

My work focuses on **API design**, **distributed workflows**, and **operational clarity**. I care about architecture decisions that keep teams fast months later, not just at launch.

## Engineering Approach

- Start from business constraints, then shape service boundaries.
- Prefer boring and observable defaults over hidden complexity.
- Design for failure: retries, idempotency, back-pressure, and graceful degradation.
- Keep data contracts explicit and versioned.

## Areas I Enjoy

- High-throughput APIs
- Event-driven services and async pipelines
- Caching strategies and latency tuning
- Search and analytics backends
- System design and architecture reviews
