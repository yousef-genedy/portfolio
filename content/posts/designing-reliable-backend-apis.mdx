Reliable APIs come from good defaults, not hero debugging.

## Practical Checklist

- Stable contracts and explicit error models
- Idempotency where duplicate writes are possible
- Timeouts and retries with bounded backoff
- Structured logs and request correlation IDs

These basics remove most production pain before it starts.
