## Context

The previous event path struggled during traffic spikes and had weak replay tooling.

## What I Built

- Partition-aware ingestion workers
- Idempotent processors with retry queues
- Redis-backed deduplication for burst protection
- Dead-letter and replay flow with operator controls

## Outcome

The platform became stable under sustained burst traffic and gave the team faster incident recovery through clear operational dashboards.
