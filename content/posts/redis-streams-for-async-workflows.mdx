Redis Streams can be a great middle ground between simple queues and heavier brokers.

## Use Cases

- Low-latency event fan-in
- Short-lived workflow coordination
- Stream consumers that need replay support

## Caveat

Choose retention and consumer-group strategy early. Operational behavior depends heavily on it.
